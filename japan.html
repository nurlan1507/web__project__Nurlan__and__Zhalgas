Tamagoyaki

Tamagoyaki (tamago-yaki) is a traditional Japanese omelette that is cooked in a special way. It is based on chicken eggs with milk and finely chopped vegetables. The omelette is prepared in thin layers, which are gradually rolled up into a roll. Japanese omelet is usually tender and juicy, and the recipe allows you to add spices, vegetables or sugar to your taste

Moti

Mochi is a type of Japanese rice dough. Sweets called "mochi" are made from it, including daifuku - cakes in the form of balls with filling. This recipe is adapted to make the Japanese mochi dessert at home. My mochi filling is strawberries and mascarpone cream with white chocolate. Mochi can be cooked in the microwave. Products (for 6 servings) For the test: Rice flour (glutinous only!) - 130 g, Powdered sugar - 80 g, Water - 150 ml, For the filling: Mascarpone cheese - 100 g, White chocolate - 50 g, Powdered sugar - 50 g, Strawberries - 6 pcs. To form mochi: Corn starch
